Engineering & Research

Much of my career has been research focused, from wave tank testing of novel wave energy converters to researching new gas models for a leading software. I like breaking down tough problems to smaller components and tackling each phase in turn.

Engineering & Design

As a mechanical engineer, I learned to design in 3D space in AutoCad, SolidWorks and PTC Creo. This means I design components with the larger system in mind. I account for technical constraints and I understand the value of testing ideas, iterating to a better solution.

Research & Design

As a UX designer, I make evidence-based design decisions. For me UX research is about asking questions, adapting to new information, and making sense of chaos. I love working with large amounts of data to identify trends that inform design.
